Common Questions

What happens when I run out of LV Tokens?
Your dashboards, data, AI narration, AI analysis, and basic queries all keep working. Only deep intelligence actions (research briefs, prospect discovery, competitive deep dives) pause until your tokens refresh next month or you grab a top-up pack. You're never locked out of your data or basic AI features.
Do unused tokens roll over?
Monthly tokens roll over for one billing cycle, capped at 2x your plan's monthly amount. Top-up tokens are valid for 60 days from purchase.
Can I access every feature on every plan?
Yes. Every plan, including Free, has access to every capability: reports, competitive intelligence, prospect discovery, playbooks, all LV Labs extensions. Plans differ by how many accounts you track and how many tokens you get each month.
Is my data import really free?
Always. Upload as many distributor files as you need. We handle the normalization. Your data is what makes the platform valuable for you, so we'll never charge for it.
What if I need more than 2,000 accounts?
Our Enterprise plan is fully custom, built for distributors and large suppliers managing national portfolios. Reach out and we'll scope it together.
