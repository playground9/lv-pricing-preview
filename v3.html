Common Questions

What's included in the 14-day trial?
Everything. Full platform access, 50 LV Tokens (covers your weekly briefs plus on-demand features), dashboards, charts, and exports. No credit card required. Your trial starts when you import your first data file, so you can take your time getting set up.
What happens after the trial ends?
Your data stays and your dashboards remain accessible. Intelligence briefs and token-powered features pause until you pick a plan. You can upgrade anytime to pick up right where you left off.
What happens when I run out of LV Tokens?
Your dashboards, data, charts, and exports keep working. AI-powered features (weekly briefs, chat, reports, discovery) pause until your tokens refresh next month or you grab a top-up pack. You're never locked out of your data.
Do unused tokens roll over?
Monthly tokens roll over for one billing cycle, capped at 2x your plan's monthly amount. Top-up tokens are valid for 60 days from purchase.
Can I access every feature on every plan?
Yes. Every paid plan has access to every capability: weekly briefs, reports, competitive intelligence, prospect discovery, playbooks, and all LV Labs extensions. Plans differ only by how many LV Tokens you get each month for on-demand actions.
Is data import really free?
Always. Upload as many distributor files as you need. We handle the normalization. Your data is what makes the platform valuable for you, so we'll never charge for it.
I'm a distributor. How does partner pricing work?
Distributors can onboard their supplier brands onto Liquid Vision at a partner discount off our standard plans. Same platform, same features, better rates. The more brands you activate, the more portfolio-level intelligence you unlock. Reach out and we'll walk through it.
